## Video direction

- palette: Bright white is the universal canvas; navy carries headlines and structure; cobalt is the only promotional accent. Existing AAC function colors appear only inside the authentic captured UI.
- typography: Segoe UI Variable Text by display/body role, with large navy display copy, compact cobalt eyebrows, and no invented font families.
- motion: One smooth camera language with long-tail power3 settles. Each surface or callout arrives on its spoken cue, especially through the back half; holds are still, with no looping breath or late camera drift.
- rhythm: Frames 2–5 are the energetic live demo run. Frame 6 is the proof hold and Frame 7 is the calm brand resolve.
- captions: Reserve the bottom 17% throughout; all product controls, highlights, labels, and headlines stay in the top 83%.
- negative: No off-brand gradients, fake UI, sports imagery, browser chrome, bouncy overshoot, slideshow front-loading, or independently floating screensaver motion.
format: 1920x1080
message: "Build a complete TD Snap topic page without editing every button by hand"
arc: "Demo Loop — promise → connect → configure → populate → review → live result → value close"
audience: "AAC users, parents, SLPs, and professionals"
mode: autonomous
music: none
