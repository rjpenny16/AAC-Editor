# TD Snap Page Builder Promo

## Style Prompt

Warm, fluid, and precise. Use the app's light Apple-inspired workspace as the visual source: generous whitespace, quiet depth, rounded controls, crisp blue actions, and human-centered copy. Motion should feel helpful and calm, with confident focus pulls and soft spatial movement.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F5F5F7`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Ink: `#1D1D1F`
- Soft ink: `#55555A`
- Action blue: `#0071E3`
- Success: `#248A3D`

## Typography

- Display and body: `Archivo`, sans-serif (open-font match for the app's system UI typography)
- Technical labels: `SF Mono`, `Cascadia Mono`, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- No neon, dark-tech, or purple-gradient styling.
- No fake product screens or invented features.
- No dense card grids or tiny web-sized text.
- No sharp, frantic, or glitchy motion.
