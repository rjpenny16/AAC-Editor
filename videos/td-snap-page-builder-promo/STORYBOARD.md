## Video direction

Use the normative `frame.md` roles: white canvas, near-black display type, soft-ink Segoe UI body, app blue as the only accent, and blue-tinted rounded cards without content shadows. Every frame keeps the bottom caption band clear and carries one thin blue progress strip.

Motion uses smooth long-tail settles and VO-paced sequential reveals: only the opening phrase enters first, with later words, cards, or UI focus cues arriving when narration names them, including the back half of each frame. Camera movement ends before the held read. Frame 5 is the deliberate quiet breather; Frame 6 resolves with a still final lockup. Never use bouncy entrances, infinite motion, lazy breathing, back-half drift, front-loaded slideshow timing, independently floating screensaver motion, invented UI states, neon effects, or extra accent colors.
